# 1. Purpose

The Industrial Brain is the central intelligence engine of TwinForge AI.

It coordinates all AI modules, Digital Twins, IoT devices, machine knowledge, historical memory, and factory operations into one unified decision-making system.

Instead of isolated AI models solving individual problems, the Industrial Brain combines their knowledge to understand the complete factory.

It becomes the "brain" of the industrial ecosystem.

---

# 2. Vision

Factories should not operate as disconnected machines.

Every machine,

Every sensor,

Every AI,

Every engineer,

Every maintenance event,

Every production line,

Every factory...

should contribute to one continuously learning intelligence platform.

This intelligence platform is the Industrial Brain.

---

# 3. Objectives

Industrial Brain shall:

• Understand factory-wide operations

• Coordinate all AI agents

• Learn continuously

• Explain every decision

• Optimize production

• Improve reliability

• Reduce energy consumption

• Protect industrial assets

• Support human engineers
