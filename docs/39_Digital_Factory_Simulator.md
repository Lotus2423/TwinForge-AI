Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Purpose

The Digital Factory Simulator (DFS) is a virtual environment where TwinForge AI can simulate industrial machines, production lines, maintenance strategies, energy consumption, and failure scenarios before they happen in the real factory.

Instead of experimenting on expensive industrial equipment, engineers can safely test ideas inside a digital environment.

---

# 2. Vision

Before making any real-world decision, factories should first test it inside a Digital Twin.

TwinForge AI becomes a "virtual factory" where every maintenance action, production change, and AI recommendation can be evaluated before execution.

---

# 3. Objectives

The Digital Factory Simulator shall:

• Simulate machine behavior

• Simulate sensor values

• Predict maintenance outcomes

• Simulate failures

• Test AI decisions

• Optimize production

• Evaluate energy usage

• Train engineers safely
