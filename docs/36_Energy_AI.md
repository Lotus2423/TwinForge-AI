# 1. Purpose

Energy AI is an intelligent optimization engine that continuously monitors, analyzes, predicts, and improves the energy efficiency of industrial machines.

Rather than simply measuring electricity consumption, Energy AI understands how machines consume power, identifies abnormal energy behavior, estimates operating costs, recommends optimizations, and supports sustainable manufacturing.

Energy AI transforms electrical data into actionable business intelligence.

---

# 2. Vision

Factories should not only know how much electricity they consume.

They should know:

• Why energy consumption changed

• Which machine wastes energy

• Which maintenance issue increases power usage

• How much money can be saved

• Which machine should be optimized first

---

# 3. Objectives

Energy AI shall:
