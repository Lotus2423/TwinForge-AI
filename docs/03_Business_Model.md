# Problem Statement

Industrial maintenance is still dominated by:

- Reactive Maintenance
- Preventive Maintenance

These approaches lead to:

- Unexpected machine failures
- High maintenance costs
- Production downtime
- Unnecessary part replacement
- Poor visibility into machine health
- Limited data-driven decision making

Many enterprise Industry 4.0 solutions are too expensive and too complex for small and medium manufacturing businesses.

---

# Our Solution

TwinForge AI provides an affordable predictive maintenance platform that combines:

- IoT Sensors
- Explainable AI
- Digital Twins
- Cloud Dashboard
- Maintenance Intelligence

The platform continuously monitors machine health, predicts failures before they occur, explains the reasons behind predictions, and recommends maintenance actions.

---

To become the preferred Industry 4.0 platform for small and medium manufacturing industries worldwide.
