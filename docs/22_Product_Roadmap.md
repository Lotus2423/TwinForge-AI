# Purpose

This document defines the long-term product vision and execution roadmap for TwinForge AI.

It outlines:

- MVP milestones
- Product evolution
- AI roadmap
- Hardware roadmap
- Startup milestones
- Business growth
- Enterprise vision

The roadmap is designed to transform TwinForge AI from a college project into a scalable Industry 4.0 SaaS platform.

---

# North Star

TwinForge AI aims to become the operating system for predictive maintenance in manufacturing.

Mission

Predict machine failures before they happen.

Explain why.

Recommend what to do.

Visualize everything through Digital Twins.
