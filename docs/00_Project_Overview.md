# Executive Summary

TwinForge AI is an Industry 4.0 platform that creates intelligent Digital Twins of industrial machines and predicts failures before they happen using Explainable Artificial Intelligence.

The platform combines Mechanical Engineering, Industrial IoT, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, and 3D Digital Twin technology into one integrated system.

Instead of performing maintenance only after machines fail, TwinForge AI continuously monitors machine health, predicts failures in advance, explains the reasons behind those predictions, and recommends maintenance actions.

The long-term objective is to build an affordable Industrial Intelligence Platform for small and medium manufacturing industries.

---

# Problem Statement

Industrial machines are commonly maintained using two approaches:

## Reactive Maintenance

Machine runs until failure.

Result:

- Unexpected downtime
- Production loss
- High repair cost
- Emergency maintenance

---

## Preventive Maintenance

Maintenance is scheduled after fixed intervals.

Result:

- Healthy parts replaced unnecessarily
- Increased maintenance cost
- Inefficient planning

---

Neither approach understands the actual health of the machine.

---

# Our Solution

TwinForge AI introduces Predictive Maintenance powered by Artificial Intelligence.

# Why TwinForge AI is Different

Traditional monitoring systems display sensor values.

TwinForge AI explains machine behaviour.

Example:

Traditional Dashboard

Temperature: 82°C

TwinForge AI

Temperature has increased continuously for six days.

Bearing vibration has increased by 42%.

The observed pattern matches historical bearing degradation.

Failure Probability: 91%

Recommended inspection within five days.
